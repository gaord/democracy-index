Democracy Index Countries by Reglion

Democracy Index was created by the Economist Intelligence Unit (http://www.eiu.com/home.aspx). It is a weighted average if indicators in the categories of electoral process and pluralism, civil liberties, functioning of government, political participation, and political culture. Using the index score, regimes are categorized as authoritarian, hybrid, flawed democracy, or full democracy.

In the visualization below, I paired the countries in Democracy Index with religious composition data from the Pew Research Center. The legend filters facilitate the comparison of religious populations to the types of regimes. The cartogram displays the geographic distribution. The axis toggle switches the y-axis from a list of religions to countries.

Answers the questions: where do people of various religions live and in what types of regimes do they have.
